What is the best time to go Paragliding in Sikkim?

The ideal time for paragliding in Sikkim is between March and June. During these months, the weather remains pleasant throughout the day, and you also witness clear skies. Another great time is September to November, between post-monsoon and pre-winters. You get clear skies and a bit of chilly weather, perfect for flying!

TIPS to consider before going

  • Stay physically fit and healthy.
  • Weight limit: under 90 kg.
  • Follow the pre-flight briefing carefully.
  • Avoid flying in strong winds or rain.
  • Inform your instructor about any medical conditions.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and sturdy shoes.
